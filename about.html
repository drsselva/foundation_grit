Our Esteemed Sponsors




Working with Govt. of Tamil Nadu


Pleased to announce that GRIT Foundation from VOC, Street, Sivaganga is working with the Department of Social Defence on two projects.

Project - 1  GRIT Foundation is conducting online NEET Preparation classes for 9th, 10th, 11th & 12th standard students' subject wise classes in all the Government Children Homes and AnnaiSathya Government Children Homes in Tamil Nadu for the academic year 2022-2023.

Project - 2   On going 1 year skill training program which include Reading, Writing, English Effective Communication skills including Personality Development classes, Leading Psychometric & Cognitive Test to bring Career Awareness and help make better career choices for the inmates (girls) of AnnaiSathya Government Children Homes Madurai, AnnaiSathya Government Children Homes Sivaganga and Government Children Home in Karaikudi. GRIT Foundation is privileged to collaborate with Department of Social Defence in doing our little in bringing positivity and great future for the Govt. Children Home Children
